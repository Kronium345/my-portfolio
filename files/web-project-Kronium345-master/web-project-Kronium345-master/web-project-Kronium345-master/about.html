About Abbeville Cafe & Bites

Opened in 2016, we provide our customers with excellent services and finest computer models.

We are Peckham's friendliest and most hospitable internet cafe.

Our computers run on a high-end EE broadband, with quick and adaptable services that allow customers to be instantly satisfied, like perfectly printed documents and files for different purposes.

Our main office is based in Peckham, London. Our reach, however, expands outside of the UK, with different services like money transfer and shippings.

This month alone we've helped over 100 customers with all of their needs, with 4 star (★★★★☆) satisfaction!

FAQs

Can I use my phone to print documents instead of the computers?

Of course! However, we do charge more to use the phone as a medium to print.

Do you sell offer lessons on how to use computers?

Yes we do. We charge 20 per session (times can be discussed on the phone, or in-person at the desk).

Do you help write out certain documents, like CVs or flyers?

Yes, we do. What you can do is leave the document that you want to type out with us for a period of time (usually 1-2 days), and we will type it out for you with perfect grammar and formatting (costs depend on the length of the document).

What are your opening hours?

Our office is open from 9.30am-8pm Monday to Saturday.

What kind of paper do you use for printing?

Normal A4 paper.
